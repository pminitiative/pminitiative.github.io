Precision Medicine Initiative in Morocco & Africa

The Precision Medicine Initiative in Morocco & Africa is an NGO that promotes and supports precision medicine programs in Morocco and Africa.

The translatinal research and developement of medical genomic discovery into actionable medical care in Morocco and Africa known as Precision Medicine is slowly adopted. However, precision medicine in Africa in general and specifically in Morocco faces significant challenges ranging from absence of legal grounds for such groundbreaking change, lack of funding, and the lack of training in bioinformatics and genetics among medical staff.

As the research and medical infrastructure in Morocco and Africa are rapidly evolving, the PM Initiative in Morocco and Africa association aims to promote and support the developement of precision medicine pilot program, participate in capacity building, and connect local reserchers with international reference collaborators.
